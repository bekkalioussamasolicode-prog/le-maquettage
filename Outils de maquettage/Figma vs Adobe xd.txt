Figma : 

Strengths :

- Excellent real-time collaboration (multiple people editing at once).

- Browser-based → works on any device, no installation needed.

- Large plugin ecosystem and strong community resources.

- Easy sharing with simple links.

Limitations :

- Requires constant internet connection for best experience.

- Can feel heavy on low-power machines.

- Some advanced prototyping still requires plugins.

Adobe XD :

Strengths :

- Very fast and lightweight performance.

- Strong integration with Adobe Creative Cloud (Photoshop, Illustrator).

- Good for simple to mid-level prototypes with smooth transitions.

Limitations :

- Collaboration features are not as advanced as Figma.

- Adobe discontinued active development in 2023 → limited future updates.

- Smaller community and fewer plugins.
